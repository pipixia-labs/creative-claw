How does Miro pricing work? ⌃
Miro offers a Free tier for individuals, Starter at $8/month, Business at $16/month, and Custom enterprise plans. Annual billing saves 15%.
Can I invite guests to my Miro workspace? ⌄
Yes — Business and Enterprise plans include day passes for visitors and external collaborators.
Is Miro SOC 2 compliant? ⌄
Yes — Miro is SOC 2 Type II certified. Enterprise plans include additional security and compliance features.
