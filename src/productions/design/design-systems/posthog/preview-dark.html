Design System Inspiration of PostHog

The dark-canvas variant of PostHog's playful developer-tools system. The cream canvas inverts to a deep olive-tinted near-black; the yellow-orange CTA pill, hedgehog mascot system, and IBM Plex Sans typography stay exactly the same. Pastel callout banners brighten to translucent-white-tinted variants for AA contrast on dark, and the code-block surface deepens one notch below canvas.

Touch Targets

  • WCAG AA: button-primary at 40px height with 16px padding.
  • Text input: 36px height.
  • Pill tab: ~32–36px extends to 44px tappable via padding.
  • Doc-sidebar items: 14px text + line-height + padding (~44px tap row).

Collapsing Strategy

  • Primary nav: desktop horizontal → tablet hamburger drawer.
  • Marketing cards: 4-up → 3-up → 2-up → 1-up.
  • Pricing grid: 3-up → 2+1 → 1-up stacked.
  • Doc layout: 240px sidebar + 720px article → tablet sidebar accordion.
  • Section padding: 80px → 64px → 48px.
