Design System / Editorial Hazard

Design System Inspiration of The Verge

The Verge's 2024 redesign uses an almost-black editorial canvas (#131313), acid-mint #3cffd0, ultraviolet #5200ff, massive Manuka display type, and rounded StoryStream tiles to create a dark, loud, magazine-meets-console interface.
