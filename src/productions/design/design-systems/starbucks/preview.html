Touch Targets

Pill buttons at `7px 16px` padding are ~32px tall — typically expanded visually on mobile to meet 44px WCAG AAA. Frap at 56px clears the minimum with an extra -0.8rem tap offset.

Collapsing Strategy

  • Global nav: 64 → 72 → 83 → 99px across breakpoints; hamburger menu below tablet
  • Hero split: 40/60 asymmetric → stacked on mobile
  • Gift-card grid: 5 → 4 → 3 → 2 → 1 columns
  • Outer gutter: 16 → 24 → 40px
  • Rewards status panels: 3-up → single column on mobile
